Jessica Hearn is a freelance writer, editor, and designer based in Minneapolis, MN.

As a writer and editor I specialize in fiction and personal memoir; as a designer I specialize in web design/development and layouts for print materials such as books, brochures, and signage. My clients have included national organizations developing new promotional materials and informational texts, as well as individuals with personal projects they wish to share primarily with friends and family. I'm always looking for new and interesting projects, and no job is too small to consider!

I'm a self-taught designer, though I'm currently working on a professional certificate in Interactive Marketing and Design through Minneapolis College of Art and Design. My bachelor's degree from New York University is in Vocal Performance, with a minor in Creative Writing, and I also hold a professional certificate in Publishing from NYU's School of Continuing and Professional Studies.
