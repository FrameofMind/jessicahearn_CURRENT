Jessica Hearn is a freelance web developer, writer, and designer based in Minneapolis, MN.

As a writer and editor I specialize in fiction and personal memoir; as a designer and developer I specialize in building HTML or Wordpress-based websites and designing layouts for print materials such as books, brochures, and signage. My clients have included national organizations developing new promotional and informational materials, as well as individuals with personal projects they wish to share primarily with friends and family. I'm always looking for new and interesting projects, and no job is too small to consider!

I'm currently finishing up a professional certificate in Interactive Marketing and Design through Minneapolis College of Art and Design. My bachelor's degree from New York University is in Vocal Performance, with a minor in Creative Writing, and I also hold a professional certificate in Publishing from NYU's School of Continuing and Professional Studies.
